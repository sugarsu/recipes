Ingredients:
*Avocados
*Tomatoes (chopped)
*White onions (chopped)
*Fresh cilantro (chopped)
*Pinch of salt

Take the skin off of the avocados and mash them in a bowl. 
Add in salt and mix the avocados until you get a creamy consistency.
Add the chopped tomatoes, onions and cilantro, and mix all together.
